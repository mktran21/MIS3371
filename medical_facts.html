Measles Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Runny nose
  • Sore throat
  • Inflamed Eyes
  • Tiny white spots with bluish-white centers on a red background found inside the mout on the inner lining of the cheek

Covid Symptoms

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Sore throat
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches

Small Pox Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Headache and Fatigue
  • Widespread skin rash-flat spots which change into raised bumps then bumps then firm fluid filled blisters which scab

Tetanus Symptoms

  • Lockjaw
  • Muscle spasms
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Difficulty breathing

Chicken Pox Symptoms

  • Rash that turns into itchy, fluid-filled blisters which eventually become scabs
